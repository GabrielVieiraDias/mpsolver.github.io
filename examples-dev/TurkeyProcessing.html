Processing Turkeys

I am having problems figuring out how to model the following problem related to cutting up turkeys in a processing plant:

We have two flocks of turkeys to process of different bird types.

  • F1 = number of birds in flock 1 of bird type 1
  • F2 = number of birds in flock 2 of bird type 2

We have 4 possible cut up processes some of which can only process birds from flock 1 (bird type 1) and some from flock 2 (bird type2) and some from either. Here are a list of the processes and the birds they can process:

  • P1 (process 1) for F1 only
  • P2 (process 2) for F1 only
  • P3 (process 3) for F1 or F2
  • P4 (process 4) for F2 only

The processes create one or more raw meats from the birds:

  • RM1 is raw meat 1
  • RM2 is raw meat 2
  • RM3 is raw meat 3
  • RM4 is raw meat 4

Here are the raw meats created from each of the process:

  • P1: x1 of RM1, x2 of RM2
  • P2: x3 of RM2, x4 of RM3, x5 of RM4
  • P3: x6 of RM1, x7 of RM4
  • P4: x8 of RM1, x9 of RM2, x10 of RM4, x11 of RM5

(where xn = the process yield per bird, so for example, x1*(#birds processed by P1) = amount of RM1 created by process P1)

We have sales requirements for the raw meats as follows:

  • S1 of RM1
  • S2 of RM2
  • S3 of RM3
  • S4 of RM4

We want the optimum processing plan to achieve the raw meats requirements from the two flocks. By optimum I mean the plan that uses the least number of birds and achieves or comes closest to achieving the sales requirements.

In my previous project we were developing a grow out plan determining the number of birds required to meet the sales requirements. lp_solve worked great for that problem. Now we want to do the reverse and find the best processing plan given a constraint on the actual number and type of birds available from the actual grow out.

I do not know how to model this for lp_solve. Any help is greatly appreciated. I hope I have stated the problem correctly. If not please let me know and I will try again.
